Oklahoma

Oklahoma lies in the central United States right between Kansas and Texas, easily recognized by its odd, cooking-pot shape. With very few large urban areas, it has become a major producer of oil, natural gas, and various agricultural products. The temperate climate gives Oklahoma a wide variety of weather through the seasons.
