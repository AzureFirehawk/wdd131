What We Do

Every time you come in for an appointment, whether it be a weekly check in or one of our classes, we will be sending you home with a pack of diapers and baby wipes. We will also include a variety of other essentials, such as bottles, clothes, and toys.

Each week we host two different classes: pregnancy class to discuss what to expect during different trimesters, and parenting classes to give tips on how to navigate the challenges of parenthood. Attendees will be given a bundle of supplies, as well as an opportunity to pick out an item from our gift room.

Our organization is only possible because of generosity from our community, and we do everything we can to make sure that generosity is paid forward. If you would like to support our cause, please contact us so we can see how you can help us make a difference.
